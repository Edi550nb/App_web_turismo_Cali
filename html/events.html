Fairs

Cali fair

The Cali Fair is one of Colombia's most iconic festivals, celebrated annually from December 25th to 30th, showcasing salsa culture, music, and local cuisine. The main event is the Salsódromo, where thousands of dancers from salsa schools parade in a spectacular show.

Cali fair 2025

The 68th edition of the Cali Fair will take place from December 25 to 30, 2025. This version, known as the Cali Fair 68, stands out for the return of its traditional events to Calle 25 and a strong focus on neighborhood culture.

Festivals

World Festival of the Living Room in Cali

The Cali World Salsa Festival is the most important salsa event in the world, held annually in September (September 25-28, 2025, for its 20th edition) at the Coliseo El Pueblo. With free admission, it brings together thousands of dancers, orchestras, and salsa lovers to celebrate the Cali style with competitions, workshops, and entrepreneurial ventures.

Image taken from:

https://www.cali.gov.co/

Petronio Álvarez Pacific Music Festival

The Petronio Álvarez Pacific Music Festival, held annually in Cali (August), is the largest Afro cultural gathering in Colombia, paying homage to Pacific music, gastronomy, crafts and traditions, named in honor of the Buenaventura composer Petronio Álvarez.

Cali International Film Festival (FICCali)

Cali International Film Festival (FICCali): This is the city's most important film event. The 2026 dates are scheduled for October 20-25.
